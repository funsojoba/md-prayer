Personal Prayers

Morning Dew Fasting and Prayer
  1. Lord, teach me to make the most of this one life that I have to your praise and glory in Jesus’ Name - Psalm 90:12.
  2. Lord, guide me into your perfect plan and purpose for me in every sphere of life in Jesus’ Name - Jer.10:23.
  3. Lord, show me the things that I ought to do to fulfill my God-given destiny in Jesus’ Name - Acts 9:6.
  4. Lord, show me my place in the Body of Christ and in your big plan for my generation in Jesus’ Name - Acts 13:2-3.
  5. Lord, strengthen my spirit and mind by the Holy Spirit to make destiny decisions that will define and redefine my life's experiences in Jesus’ Name - Eph. 3:14-20.
  6. Lord, reveal yourself to me and show me who I am in Christ that I may walk worthy of You and maximize my potentials in Christ in Jesus’ Name - Eph. 1:15-19.
  7. Lord, connect me with the people that matter in the fulfillment of my destiny in Jesus’ Name - Phil.4:3; 1 Sam. 18:1-3.
  8. Lord, order my steps in Your Word in Jesus’ Name – Psa. 119:133.
  9. Lord, help me understand the different times and seasons of my life in Jesus’ Name - Eccl. 3:1; 1 Chr. 12:32.
  10. Lord, help me recognise the opportunities You bring my way and teach me to use them for my benefit and for Your glory in Jesus’ Name - Eccl.9:11; Eph.5:16; Col.4:5.
  11. Lord, I am the seed of Abraham; teach me to give expression to the blessing that I am in Christ that I might extend Your blessing to my world in Jesus’ Name - Gal.3:13; Rom.4:14-16.
  12. Lord, I am the light of this world; teach me to connect the world with the light that is in me in Jesus’ Name - Matt. 5:13-16.
  13. Lord, keep me from the error of dishonour; by Your grace, I will not dishonour whatever and whosoever on whom You have placed Your honour in Jesus’ Name - 2 Pet. 2:10; Psalm 19:13-14.
  14. Lord, keep me from every evil and guide my feet in the path of peace in Jesus’ Name - Jude 1:24; Rom. 15:33; 1 Thess. 5:23.
  15. Lord, You’ve provided all that I need for life and godliness in Christ Jesus; therefore, show me how to make the most of Your grace in Jesus’ Name - Eph.1:17:21; 2 Pet. 1:3.
